# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for
everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics,
gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic
status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and empathetic.
- Focus criticism on ideas and code, not people.
- Accept responsibility, apologize when appropriate, and learn from mistakes.
- Respect privacy and avoid publishing another person's private information.

Harassment, sexualized attention, threats, insults, trolling, and sustained
disruption are unacceptable in project spaces and when representing the project.

## Enforcement

Report abusive behavior privately to the project maintainers. Reports will be
reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as possible. Maintainers may edit,
remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently ban participants
whose behavior is inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
